Frequently Asked Questions About Shopify Costs

Monthly Shopify costs start at 33 € for the Basic plan. The Grow plan costs 88 €/month, the Advanced plan 384 €. Transaction fees per order plus optional apps, agency, or POS Pro add to your running costs. Use our Shopify cost calculator to estimate your total.

For solopreneurs and small shops, the Basic plan usually suffices. With growing volume, the Grow plan offers lower transaction fees. The Advanced plan suits high-volume businesses. Our cost calculator recommends a plan based on your inputs.

Running Shopify costs include: plan fee, transaction fees (depending on payment provider and plan), optional apps, agency support, and Shopify POS Pro for in-store sales. Small shops often pay 150–400 €/month in total.

With Shopify Payments: Basic 2.9 % + €0.30, Grow 2.7 % + €0.30, Advanced 2.5 % + €0.30 per transaction. External providers (PayPal, Klarna) add 2 % (Basic), 1 % (Grow), or 0.5 % (Advanced). Our calculator shows exact fees based on your payment mix.

Yes. The Basic plan at 33 €/month suits beginners. Hosting, SSL, updates, and support are included – with open-source solutions these running costs often apply separately. Use our cost calculator to plan your budget.

Shopify Plus starts at around 2,300 €/month for high-volume businesses. It includes advanced B2B features, custom checkout, priority support, and more. You can enable Shopify Plus in our cost calculator to include it in your planning.

Main costs are transparent: plan price and transaction fees. Additional expenses may come from apps, premium themes, agency fees, or Shopify POS Pro. Our cost calculator includes these optional items so you can plan your total running costs realistically.
