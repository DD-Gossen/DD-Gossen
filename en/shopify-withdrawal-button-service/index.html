Frequently asked questions

What exactly does the package cost?

A one-time fee of €150 plus VAT. No monthly follow-up costs, no app subscriptions. The invoice is issued only after successful implementation and testing.

When is the invoice issued?

The invoice is issued after the implementation is completed and successfully tested, so only once everything is working.

What do I need to get started?

Enter your shop URL, sender email for withdrawal confirmations, contact email, and billing address in the order form. I'll ask you by email for the four-digit collaborator request code from Shopify Admin (Settings → Users and Permissions → Collaborator requests) after your inquiry.

How does the shop access work?

Via Shopify Collaborator access. That is Shopify's official partner access method. You don't share your admin login; instead you grant a targeted, time-limited access that can be revoked at any time.

Which stores is this suitable for?

The package is suitable for all Shopify stores selling B2C within the EU. Requirements: a current Shopify theme (Online Store 2.0 recommended) and an active Shopify plan.

Is there more information about the technical setup?

Yes. Our step-by-step guide covers the complete technical implementation, for those who want to do it themselves.
