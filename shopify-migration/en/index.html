Shopify Migration: Why Switching to Shopify Is Worth It

Shopify offers a stable, secure and scalable e-commerce platform. Compared to self-hosted systems like WooCommerce or Shopware, you eliminate server maintenance, security updates and plugin conflicts. An experienced Shopify freelancer ensures the migration runs smoothly and without data loss.

When migrating from WooCommerce to Shopify, products, customers and orders are systematically exported and imported into Shopify. Particularly important: 301 redirects for existing URLs to preserve your Google rankings. As a Shopify freelancer, I handle the entire process.
